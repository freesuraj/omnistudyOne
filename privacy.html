Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2026

Overview

OmniStudy helps you transform documents into quizzes, flashcards, summaries, and insights. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the OmniStudy iOS app and this website.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as your email, uploaded documents, and study preferences. We also collect app usage analytics and device information to improve performance and reliability.

How We Use Information

We use your information to generate study content, provide support, personalize your learning experience, and improve the app. Documents are processed securely and only used to deliver the features you request.

Document Processing

Uploaded documents are analyzed to create quizzes, flashcards, and summaries. We use trusted AI infrastructure to process content. We do not sell your documents or use them for advertising.

Data Retention

Your documents and generated study materials are retained as long as your account is active or until you delete them. You may request deletion at any time.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data in transit and at rest. No system is perfectly secure, but we continuously improve safeguards and access controls.

Sharing

We share data only with service providers necessary to run the app, such as hosting, analytics, and AI processing services. These providers are required to protect your information.

Your Choices

You can access, update, or delete your account data within the app. If you need help, contact support using the information below.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at support@omnistudy.app.
